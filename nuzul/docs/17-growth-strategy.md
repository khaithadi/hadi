# 17 — Marketplace Growth Strategy

## The liquidity problem
Two-sided marketplace: solve **supply first** in a city, then drive demand, then repeat.
Cold-start with concierge-onboarded hosts before paid demand.

## Supply (hosts)
- **Concierge onboarding** of existing Facebook-group/classified hosts (we list for them).
- Free **professional photography** + free **Featured** for early hosts.
- **0% commission launch window** per new wilaya for the first N bookings.
- Property-manager partnerships for instant multi-unit supply.

## Demand (guests)
- City-by-city SEO (wilaya landing pages already prerendered) + Google/Meta.
- **Diaspora** targeting abroad pre-summer (FR/EN ads, "book before you fly home").
- Influencer/UGC on Instagram/TikTok; WhatsApp broadcast lists.

## Retention & loops
- **Reviews** flywheel (trust → conversion → more bookings → more reviews).
- Referral credits (guest & host), repeat-stay discounts, wishlist re-engagement push.
- WhatsApp re-marketing (highest open rates locally).

## Trust as growth
"Verified, scam-free, deposit-protected" is the wedge vs Facebook. Verification badges and
visible reviews directly lift conversion.

## Metrics that matter
Active listings/wilaya, search→book, host response rate, repeat rate, NPS, GMV, take-rate.
North-star: **completed stays per month**.
