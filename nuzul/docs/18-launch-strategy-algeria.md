# 18 — Launch Strategy — Algeria

## Beachhead
Launch where demand + supply concentrate: **Alger (16), Tipaza (42), Oran (31), Béjaïa (6),
Annaba (23)** — coastal/summer corridor — then Tizi Ouzou, Constantine, Ghardaïa (desert
tourism), Blida/Chréa (mountain).

## Sequencing
1. **Pre-launch (6–8 wks):** concierge-onboard 150–300 hosts in Alger/Tipaza; seed photos &
   calendars; recruit local ops.
2. **Soft launch:** invite-only guests, WhatsApp support, gather reviews, fix funnel.
3. **City launch:** PR + influencers + diaspora ads timed to **spring (pre-summer)**.
4. **Roll forward** wilaya-by-wilaya once liquidity thresholds hit.

## Local adaptation
- **Payments:** deposit online (BaridiMob/Edahabia/CIB) + cash/transfer balance; diaspora
  cards via international rail.
- **Language:** Arabic-first RTL + French + English; Darija search synonyms.
- **Comms:** WhatsApp as primary support + notification channel; SMS fallback.
- **Regulation:** short-term-rental/tourism licensing guidance for hosts, tax receipts, guest
  registration where required, Law 18-07 data compliance.
- **Behaviour:** Android-dominant, data-cost-sensitive → lean PWA, offline shell, small images.

## Seasonality
August diaspora peak, religious holidays (Eid), summer coast, winter mountain/desert. Plan
inventory drives & pricing nudges around these.

## GTM channels
SEO (wilaya pages), Meta/Google, TikTok/Instagram creators, diaspora Facebook groups,
university & event partnerships, tourism agencies.

## Launch KPIs
Listings/wilaya ≥ 200 before demand spend; search→book ≥ 4%; host response ≤ 2h; CAC payback
< 2 bookings.
