# 19 — Monetization Strategy

## Revenue streams
| Stream | Mechanism | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Commission** | host take-rate (default 12% of nightly) | primary; configurable per scope |
| **Service fee** | guest fee (default 5%) | embedded in deposit capture |
| **Featured listings** | hosts pay for placement/boost | `FeaturedListing` window + price |
| **Advertising** | home banners / in-search | `Ad` placements, targeted |
| **Host subscriptions** | Free / Pro / Agency | lower fees + tools at higher tiers |
| **Photography** | paid professional shoots | also a supply-quality lever |
| **Verification services** | paid host/listing verification | trust + revenue |
| **Add-ons (phase 2+)** | insurance, damage protection, cleaning, installments | per-booking upsells |

## Pricing strategy
- Per-night in **DZD**, host-set; platform adds transparent service fee (shown in widget &
  the "price explanation" surface).
- **Deposit rate** tuned to balance conversion vs no-shows (default 20%).
- **Launch incentives:** 0% commission window per new wilaya; free Featured for early hosts.

## Leakage control (critical)
Offline balance invites off-platform deals. Counter with: deposit protection, instant
refunds, verified-review gating, loyalty credits, in-app-only support/dispute resolution,
and penalties for circumvention.

## Unit economics (illustrative)
Avg booking 25,000 DZD → commission 3,000 + service fee 1,250 ≈ **17% blended take**, minus
payment + ops cost. Featured/ads/subscriptions are high-margin overlays.
